For delegates

Tabs

  • Resolutions: all approved resolutions.
  • International Summit: one promoted submission per committee.
  • My Committee: your chairs, agenda, fellow delegations, and queued resolutions.
  • Note Passing: send and receive notes within your committee.
  • Upload: submit a new resolution.
  • Schedule: the conference programme.
  • Settings: profile, contact info, password.

Submitting a resolution

  1. Open Upload.
  2. Pick your agenda item.
  3. Title it using the conference format, e.g. UNIDO - 1.1.
  4. Paste the Google Doc URL (set to Anyone with the link · Viewer).
  5. Optionally attach a PDF copy.
  6. Submit. The resolution stays hidden until your chair approves it.

Proposing amendments

  • Open the resolution; use the Amendments panel on the right.
  • Fill in Location (e.g. 3, a, ii) and the amendment body.
  • Submit. Amendments cannot be edited or deleted, so be precise.

Voting

  • When the chair opens voting, green For and red Against buttons appear.
  • You can change your vote until the chair locks the count.
  • Once locked, the tally is final.

Note passing

  • Pick a recipient country or Chairboard.
  • Notes stay within your committee.
  • Every note is visible to the chairs.

Settings

  • Participant name is admin-set. Ask an admin to fix typos.
  • Email and phone are yours to edit.
  • Changing your password signs out your other devices.

For chairs

Everything a delegate can do, plus the following.

Approving resolutions

  • New uploads land in My Committee under "Awaiting approval".
  • Open, review, and click Approve and publish.

Resolution status

  • Four states: Pending, Debated, Passed, Failed.
  • Set via the status row at the top of the resolution.
  • Locking a vote sets the status automatically.

Voting controls

  • Open voting when the committee is ready.
  • Reset wipes votes; Reopen unlocks a locked vote.
  • Lock vote & count freezes the tally. Ties resolve as Debated.

International Summit

  • Promote one approved resolution per committee.
  • Picking a new one withdraws the previous.

Amendments

  • All amendments for a resolution sit inside that resolution's page.
  • You cannot edit or delete amendments. Discuss them in debate.

Tally Tracker

  • Click +POI (1), +Speech (2), or +Amendment (2) next to a delegation.
  • Use Undo to remove a misclick.

Notes

  • You see every note in your committee.
  • You can send notes as Chair.
  • Chairboard notes are visible only to chairs and admins.

For admins

Full visibility across all committees.

User management

  • Open Users. Create with username, display name, participant name, password, role, committee, delegation.
  • Delegates require a delegation; chairs and admins do not.
  • Reset changes a password and revokes other sessions.
  • Delete removes the user. Their amendments and resolutions remain; their notes are removed.

Note passing

  • You can send into any committee.
  • The recipient sees the sender as "Admin".

Maintenance

  • Restart: lsof -ti:5050 | xargs kill, then python3 app.py.
  • Database: data/app.db. Back it up before each conference.
  • Uploads: uploads/.
  • Network: set ALLOWED_IP_PREFIXES in .env.
